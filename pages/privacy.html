Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy and the confidentiality of your documents. Learn how we securely handle your data when you use our printing and stationery services.

The Short Version

We do not sell your personal data. Any documents you share for printing are kept strictly confidential and are only retained for the duration needed to complete your print job. After your job is done, your files are secure.

1. Document Privacy & Confidentiality

We understand that the documents you bring to us for printing (legal papers, college projects, personal letters) contain sensitive information.

  • Strict Confidentiality: Our staff will not read, copy, or distribute the contents of your documents to any third party.
  • File Retention: Files sent to us via email, pen drive, or WhatsApp are only kept temporarily to facilitate your print job. We periodically clear our download folders to protect your privacy.

2. Information Shared via WhatsApp

When you use the WhatsApp feature on our website to send files or ask for quotations:

  • Direct Communication: You are communicating directly with our shop's official business number.
  • Data Safety: We do not use your phone number for spam marketing campaigns. We will only contact you regarding your active orders or direct inquiries.

3. Website Usage & Cookies

Our website (virarprint.in) is primarily informational and does not require you to create an account or log in.

  • No Tracking: We do not use invasive third-party ad-tracking cookies.
  • Local Storage: We may use basic local storage (in your browser) to remember simple preferences, like saving your recent searches in our Search Bar or remembering if you dismissed a banner. This data never leaves your device.

4. Contact Us Regarding Privacy

If you have any specific concerns about how a highly sensitive document will be handled (e.g., legal affidavits or company confidential files), please let us know beforehand.
