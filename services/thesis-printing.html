Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I send my thesis or project on WhatsApp for printing?

Yes! Simply WhatsApp your PDF or Word file to +91 70210 72757. We'll confirm the page count, note the colour pages, and confirm the cost before we print — no surprises.

Can you handle same-day printing for a 100+ page thesis?

Absolutely. We regularly print theses of 100 to 300+ pages the same day. For very urgent or large orders (500+ pages), WhatsApp or call ahead so we can prioritise your job.

What binding options are available for my thesis?

We offer spiral binding (₹30) with a transparent front cover and black back card — accepted by most colleges. Hardbound binding with a hard cover is also available on request for final-year projects. Call us for hardbound pricing.

Can I get colour cover pages mixed with B&W body pages?

Yes! This is the most popular choice for student projects. Colour pages (₹10 each) for the cover and charts, B&W (₹3 each) for text-heavy pages. Just indicate the colour pages in your file when you send it.
