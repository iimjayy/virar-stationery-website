Frequently Asked Questions

What paper sizes do you print?

We offer printing on A4 and A3 paper in both black & white and full colour. A2 prints are also available on request.

How do I send my file for printing?

Simply WhatsApp your file (PDF, Word, PowerPoint, or image) to +91 70210 72757. We accept files via WhatsApp, USB drive, or email.

How fast is your printing service?

Most standard print jobs are ready within minutes. For bulk orders of 200+ pages, we typically complete them the same day — call ahead for large jobs.

Can I get a bulk discount on printing?

Yes, we offer special pricing for bulk orders of 100 pages or more. WhatsApp us with your page count and requirements for a custom quote.
