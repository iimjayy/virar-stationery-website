Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

What is spiral binding?

Spiral binding uses a metal or plastic coil threaded through punched holes along the edge of your document. It lets the document lay flat when open — perfect for projects, reports, and manuals. It's the most popular binding choice for college submissions.

Is a transparent cover included?

Yes! Every spiral-bound document comes with a clear transparent front cover so your title page is visible, plus a black card back cover — both included at no extra charge.

How long does binding take for large projects?

Standard projects of 50–100 pages are bound in under 10 minutes. Larger projects of 200+ pages take 20–30 minutes. Same-day service is always guaranteed — call or WhatsApp us before arriving if you have an urgent deadline.

Do you do blackbook binding for engineering projects?

Yes! We handle blackbook binding for BE, MCA, and other engineering final-year projects. Blackbooks feature hard black covers with gold or silver lettering. Please WhatsApp or call us in advance to schedule blackbook binding.
