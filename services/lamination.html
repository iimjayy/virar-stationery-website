Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

What sizes do you laminate?

We laminate A4 (₹10), A3 (₹20), and A2 (₹35) size documents. Smaller custom sizes such as visiting card and ID card sizes are also available — just ask us.

What is the difference between glossy and matte lamination?

Glossy lamination gives a shiny, vibrant finish — great for photos and award certificates. Matte lamination is non-reflective and looks more professional — ideal for ID cards, menus, and formal documents. Both are available at the same price.

How long does lamination take?

Most lamination jobs are completed in under 5 minutes. You can walk in during your lunch break and be done before you know it.

Can you laminate certificates and important ID cards?

Yes! We regularly laminate mark sheets, degree certificates, award letters, Aadhaar cards, PAN cards, and school or college ID cards to keep them safe from damage and moisture.
