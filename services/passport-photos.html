Frequently Asked Questions

What are the specifications of your passport size photos?

Our passport photos are 35mm × 45mm — the standard Indian passport size — printed on high-quality glossy photo paper. They meet the photo requirements for all Indian government applications including passport, PAN, Aadhaar, and visa forms.

How many photos do I get in one set?

Each set costs ₹30 and includes 20 passport size photos — giving you enough copies for multiple applications, forms, and records at one time.

Can I get a soft copy or digital copy of my photo?

Yes! We provide a soft copy (digital file) of your passport photo on WhatsApp at no extra charge. Just let us know when collecting your photos.

What background colour is used for passport photos?

We use a white background by default, which is accepted for most Indian government documents and visa applications. A light blue background is also available on request at no extra charge.
