Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

What sizes can I get xeroxed?

We offer A4 and A3 xerox in both black & white and full colour. Bring in any standard document and we'll copy it instantly.

Do you offer discounted bulk xerox pricing?

Yes! For bulk xerox orders of 100 pages or more, we offer special discounted rates. WhatsApp us with your requirements and we'll provide a quote.

How quickly can you complete a xerox job?

Single pages and small batches are done within seconds. Large bulk jobs of several hundred pages are typically completed the same day — call ahead if you have a very large order.

Can you xerox government documents like Aadhaar or PAN?

Absolutely. We regularly photocopy Aadhaar cards, PAN cards, passports, voter ID cards, ration cards, and other government-issued documents.
