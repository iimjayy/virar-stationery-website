Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy and handle your print files and personal information with care. This policy outlines how Virar Stationery & Jumbo Xerox collects and protects your data.

Last Updated: May 2026

1. Customer Print Files & Documents

As a printing business, we frequently handle sensitive personal and professional documents (such as Aadhar cards, project reports, and legal papers).

  • Strict Confidentiality: Your uploaded or shared files are used strictly for printing purposes.
  • No Unauthorized Sharing: We do not share, distribute, or read the contents of your personal files.
  • Data Deletion: Files shared via WhatsApp or email are kept only as long as necessary to complete your print order and are periodically cleared from our systems.

2. WhatsApp & Contact Form Data

When you contact us for a quote or query via WhatsApp, phone, or our website's contact form, we collect basic information such as your name and phone number.

  • We use this information only to respond to your enquiries, provide service updates, and share quotations.
  • We do not send spam or unsolicited promotional messages to your personal WhatsApp numbers.

3. Cookies & Analytics

Our website uses minimal, standard cookies and analytics tools (like Google Analytics) to ensure the site functions properly and to help us understand how visitors use our website.

These tools collect aggregated, non-personally identifiable information, such as which pages are visited most often, helping us improve the browsing experience.

4. Third-Party Integrations

Our website utilizes third-party tools such as Google Maps for directions and WhatsApp for direct messaging.

When you interact with these features, you are subject to the privacy policies of those respective platforms. We recommend reviewing their policies if you have concerns about how they handle data.

5. Contact Us About Your Privacy

If you have any concerns regarding how we handle your documents or personal data, please do not hesitate to contact us at the shop directly.
